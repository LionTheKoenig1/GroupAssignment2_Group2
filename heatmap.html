Game Review Visualization

Group 2

This adjacency map / heatmap presents is created from
the review data of the game with the Steam App ID .

The darker the cell, the more does this word combination
appeard in positive reviews or negative reviews

Interactivity:

The matrix can be zoomed and dragged with a mouse or with a touchscreen.
The labels on the top and left scale and move along with the cells.

Hovering over a cell shows a tooltip.
